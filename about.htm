About Me

Hi Welcome to my Website!

"Hello, I'm Reynaldo Guiao IV, a licensed architect with a passion for sustainable design and innovative building solutions. With over 10 years of experience in the industry, I have worked on a wide range of projects, from residential homes to commercial high-rises. I hold a Bachelor of Architecture degree from Holy Angel University and I am a member of the Pampanga Institute of Architects (PIA). My skills and expertise include proficiency in Autodesk Revit, SketchUp, and Adobe Creative Suite, as well as experience with building information modeling (BIM) and construction management.
